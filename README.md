Your Guider 🚀

AI-Powered Career Guidance Platform for Students After 12th

📌 Problem Statement

Har saal lakhon students 12th complete karne ke baad confusion face karte hain:

Engineering ya Medical?
Government Job ya Private Sector?
Degree ya Skill-Based Career?
Kis field me future opportunities zyada hain?

Galat career choice ki wajah se students time, money aur motivation dono lose kar dete hain.

💡 Solution

Your Guider ek AI-powered career guidance platform hai jo students ko unke interests, strengths aur goals ke basis par suitable career paths suggest karta hai.

Platform personalized recommendations aur step-by-step roadmaps provide karta hai taaki students informed decisions le saken.

🚀 How It Works

Step 1

Student registration/login karta hai.

Step 2

Interest-based questions answer karta hai.

Step 3

AI profile analyze karti hai.

Step 4

Best career options recommend kiye jaate hain.

Step 5

Student detailed roadmap explore karta hai aur future planning start karta hai.
